Founder's Day

Dr. Amita Chauhan ma'am is the chairperson of Amity International School. Her birthday is celebrated every year on 27th April as Founder's Day. She is our inspiration, mentor and mother.

She told amitians about BHAAG; a mantra of success.

B-Bheaviour

H-Hardwork

A-Ambition

A-Attitude

G-God

The mantra of BHAAG has inspired Amitians and helped them to reach out to the stars.

Ma'am always motivates amitians with her favourite lines.
